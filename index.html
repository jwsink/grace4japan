Our Story

The Sinks arrived in Nagoya, Japan in 2012 with their sons Joshua, Josiah, and Garrett. They work closely with an international church plant called All Nations Fellowship where Jeremy previously served as a church planter and the lead pastor before passing that baton to one of ANF’s former Japanese interns.  Now, the Sinks lead a new team devoted to coming alongside Japanese pastors and their wives with the goal of facilitating new church planting in Japan.  The Sinks long to see these Japanese leaders thriving and succeeding in their callings to reach their own people with the Gospel.  To that end, they mentor, coach, and come alongside these leaders and their wives who are often serving in isolation and with little support.  The work is hard, but the Sinks are encouraged by how the Spirit is working in Nagoya and all over Japan.

Our Team

With less than 1% of the population professing faith in Christ, the Japanese represent one of the largest unreached people groups in the world. The city of Nagoya is strategically located in central Japan between the major cities of Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka.

With an established local seminary and recent progress among local church planters, Nagoya is a prime base for the development of future leaders who will carry the Gospel into new areas of Japan.
